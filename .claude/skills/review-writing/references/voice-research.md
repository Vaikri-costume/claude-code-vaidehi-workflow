# Review Writing — Voice Consistency: Research Appendix

## Agent D — Voice consistency: research register

In research essays, flag:
- Passages where the writer defers entirely to a source's framing without adding their own analytical layer
- Shifts from analytical argument to bibliographic description ("Scholar X wrote... Scholar Y argued... Scholar Z showed...")
- Passages where hedging is so heavy that the writer's actual position is unreadable

Do NOT flag appropriate disciplinary hedging of interpretive claims. Flag only where the hedging has replaced the analytical move rather than qualified it.
