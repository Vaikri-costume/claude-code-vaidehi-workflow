# Per-Source Brief — Personal/Application Essay Additions

Appended to per-source.md for personal, application, and other non-research essays.

---

## Source material scope

Personal essays typically have no Logseq annotation database. Per-source checks only run if the user has explicitly provided reference file paths. If no source material was provided, this run is voice-only — only the voice agent applies.

---

## Check 2 adaptation — Reflection vs narrative

"Synthesis vs summary" applies differently in personal writing. The equivalent check is: has the writer reflected on their experience and drawn something from it, or are they merely describing what happened?

**Flag when:** A paragraph recounts events or experiences in sequence without the writer's reflective voice — what they understood, felt, or changed about their thinking. Pure narrative without reflection is the personal-essay equivalent of summary.

**Retain when:** The narrative serves a purpose that the surrounding paragraphs make clear — sometimes showing without telling is the right move. Only flag when reflection is absent from the essay section as a whole.
